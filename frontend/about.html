Our Mission

Currently, 30% of inner-city traffic is caused by people looking for parking. We saw this problem daily—wasted fuel, wasted time, and unnecessary stress.

We imagine a city where you’re never more than 500–1000 meters away from an organized parking space—equipped with EV charging, safety support, and mobility services. Park Conscious is not just an app; it's an infrastructure layer for the smart cities of tomorrow.
